FAQ:

  • How do I become a Template Creator?

    First, you must take a brief course and pass our DocuSign Assessment: here. Once completed, email us at: docusign@fiu.edu to get in touch with our DocuSign team and we'll work with you to get you the DocuSign role you need.

  • How do I send documents through DocuSign?

    To send documents through DocuSign you will need to be a DocuSign Sender. Once you have the DocuSign sender role, refer to the senders area for instructions.

  • What is a DocuSign template?

    A Template is a reusable envelope that can be sent out to users as needed. This feature is best for recurring types of documents or when you need to send out the same envelope to different parties at a later date.

  • What is a DocuSign PowerForm?

    A PowerForm is a reusable form you can host on a website or distribute as a secured link through email. Signers can access and sign PowerForms on demand. PowerForms are a sure way to capture signatures and data from persons whom you do not know beforehand.

  • How do I prevent incomplete documents?

    When creating a form, Senders or Template Creators can mark any fields as “required.” This helps ensure documents are filled out completely. DocuSign will not allow a Signer to return the document until all required fields are complete. It’s also recommended to inform Signers of any additional documents needed before sending the PowerForm link.

  • How can I tell if an email I receive asking me to sign a document is safe?

    A valid DocuSign email includes an alternate signing method with a security code you can enter at DocuSign.com. If unsure, visit the site, click “Access Documents,” and paste the security code to access your document. For more info, visit DocuSign’s incident reporting page.

  • Do people signing documents need to request access to DocuSign?

    No. Only Template or PowerForm creators need a specific role. FIU users authenticated through FIU Single Sign-On can sign and send documents without requesting access.

  • Is there DocuSign training available?

    Yes. Please visit our resources page: here. You’ll find training for Signers, Senders, and Template Creators.

  • When and how should I create Templates?

    If you send the same document frequently, create a Template. For instructions, refer to the Template Creator page.

  • Where should Signers store copies of signed documents?

    DocuSign stores FIU documents securely. Local backups are not required.

  • Does DocuSign have a storage limit or storage capacity?

    DocuSign provides FIU with ample storage. Documents are archived securely.

  • Why am I having problems uploading a document to DocuSign?

    Fillable PDFs or Active XFA forms often fail to upload. To fix:

    1. Open the form in Adobe Acrobat.
    2. Print to PDF using Acrobat's print menu.
    3. Upload the new “printed” file to DocuSign.

    If your file is over 5MB:

    1. Open in Adobe Acrobat Pro.
    2. Use Save As and choose "Reduced Size PDF" or "Optimized PDF."

    Still having trouble? Contact FIU DocuSign support.

  • Can I change the default email notifications on DocuSign?

    Yes. Go to Preferences → Manage Email Notifications to adjust settings.

  • Can I change a standard form or Template?

    Changes to standard forms or templates must be requested through the responsible Template Creator.

  • What does it mean to have an access code for a Template?

    Access codes are required for documents with confidential or restricted data sent outside FIU. SMS codes are also available for added security.

  • What is a DocuSign tag?

    Tags highlight areas in a document where action is required — e.g., Signature, Date Signed, Check Box.

  • Are templates ADA Compliant?

    DocuSign includes features and best practices to ensure accessibility and ADA compliance.

  • Will all future Templates be reviewed?

    Template Creators must follow FIU or department guidelines. Only PowerForms are subject to formal review.

  • Can you change Template owners?

    Yes. Contact FIU DocuSign support to request a change in template ownership.

  • How does Template sharing work?

    Template Creators can share templates with others if they have sharing permissions. You can also adjust sharing settings for templates shared with you. Contact support for help with share settings.

  • Can we upload PDFs and attach tags to them?

    Yes.

  • Can I send a document to someone outside FIU?

    Yes.

  • Can I send the same document to multiple recipients at the same time?

    Yes. Use the Add Bulk Recipient button on the Send screen and upload a CSV file. For more help, see the New DocuSign Experience User Guide.

  • Can I send the same document again?

    Yes. Go to the original envelope on the Manage screen and click Clone from the Actions list.

  • Do non-employees need a DocuSign account to sign FIU documents?

    No. Non-employees do not need a DocuSign account to sign.

  • Can I share my inbox and sent folder to personnel on my team?

    Yes. DocuSign’s sharing feature lets others take action on your folders. Contact support and provide the names of team members for access.
